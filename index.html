Projects

Alphabetic Sign Language Translator
for Online Meetings

In today's hybrid world, effective communication is essential. Our alphabetic sign language translator, seamlessly integrated into the Cisco Webex meeting platform, addresses the challenge of language barriers in virtual meetings. By recognizing and translating hand gestures in real-time, this innovative solution generates subtitles directly on the meeting screen. Supporting English and Chinese characters, it empowers individuals with hearing impairments to fully participate in virtual meetings and fosters seamless collaboration among diverse teams.
